UNDERSTANDING THE CHALLENGE

WHAT IS DRUG ABUSE

Drug abuse affects all aspects of life, from physical and mental health to relationships and opportunities. Understanding its causes and effects is the first step toward prevention and recovery.

UNDERSTANDING ADDICTION

Addiction is a complex disease that affects brain function and behavior. It's characterized by compulsive substance use despite harmful consequences.

THE RISKS

Drug abuse carries immediate and long-term health risks, including organ damage, mental health disorders, increased risk of accidents, and overdose.

PATH TO RECOVERY

Recovery is possible with proper support. Treatment options include therapy, medication, support groups, and holistic approaches tailored to individual needs.

RECOVERY STORIES
